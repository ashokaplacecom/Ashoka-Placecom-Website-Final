We are the Placement Committee at Ashoka University — connecting students with opportunity, ambition with mentorship, and vision with tangible outcomes.

About Us

The Placement Committee at Ashoka University serves as a link between students, recruiters, and the Career Development Office (CDO).
As an organization, our mission is to encourage professional growth and enable career success for each student.

EMPOWER

We prepare Ashokans for the future by facilitating access to internships, placements, and networking opportunities.

ENGAGE

We actively engage with firms, recruiters, and alumni to build bridges between academia and industry.

ENABLE

We strive to create a supportive ecosystem that enables every student to pursue their career goals confidently.
